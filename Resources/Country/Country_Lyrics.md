# Country Lyrics Playbook

## Objective
Tell specific, emotionally truthful stories with strong imagery, plainspoken language, and memorable hooks.

## Core Principles
- Start with a concrete setting: road, bar, church, porch, truck, county line.
- Pair universal emotions with specific details.
- Favor conversational lines that sound natural when spoken.
- Land the title in the chorus with maximum emotional clarity.

## Lyric Strategies
- Verse: scene + character detail.
- Chorus: central truth, often with a twist on the title.
- Verse 2: raise stakes or reveal backstory.
- Bridge: perspective shift, confession, or hard-earned insight.

## LLM Writing Guidance
Request:
- Title list with hook logic (double meaning, irony, phrase flip).
- Story outline before full lyrics.
- “Traditional” and “modern country-pop” language variants.
- Alternate bridge with stronger emotional turn.

## Quality Checklist
- Do details feel lived-in and believable?
- Is the chorus singable after one pass?
- Does the story progress across verses?
