# Country Harmony Playbook

## Objective
Support storytelling with familiar, emotionally direct harmonic movement.

## Core Principles
- Keep progressions accessible and vocalist-friendly.
- Let melody and lyric drive perceived sophistication.
- Use harmonic lift in choruses rather than constant complexity.

## Common Progressions
- I–V–vi–IV (modern crossover)
- I–IV–V (traditional backbone)
- vi–IV–I–V (heartland ballad feel)
- I–V/vi–vi–IV (bass movement for momentum)

## LLM Composition Guidance
Ask for:
- Acoustic-first chord chart + capo suggestions.
- Verse/chorus contrast with optional pre-chorus lift.
- Pedal steel/fiddle-friendly voicing hints.
- Traditional vs. contemporary harmonic versions.

## Quality Checklist
- Is the progression easy to strum and sing live?
- Does chorus harmony feel like emotional payoff?
- Are substitutions tasteful and genre-consistent?
