# Producer Insight: Herbie Hancock on Improvisation

## Core Lesson
Treat mistakes as new pathways; listening and responsiveness define great improvisation.

## Actionable Takeaways
- Reframe "wrong notes" as tension opportunities.
- Prioritize deep listening across collaborators.
- Use contrast and surprise to maintain interest.

## LLM Application
- Ask for experimental alternates, then curated refinements.
- Generate variation sets with increasing risk levels.
- Keep one "safe" and one "adventurous" option in every draft cycle.
