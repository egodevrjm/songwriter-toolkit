# Jazz Lyrics Playbook

## Mission
Write lyrically elegant lines that tolerate phrasing freedom, swing, and reinterpretation.

## Best Practices
- Favor imagistic specificity with emotional subtext.
- Build lines with rhythmic elasticity.
- Use repeated phrases that gain new meaning on return.

## LLM Workflow
- Generate lyric in strict meter + flexible performance variant.
- Provide optional scat placeholders and pickup lines.
- Offer late-night club vs. concert-hall tone variants.

## Evaluation Rubric
- Interpretive flexibility
- Diction singability
- Emotional layering depth
