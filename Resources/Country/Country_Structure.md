# Country Structure Playbook

## Mission
Create narrative-first structures that keep hooks central and deliver a strong final tag.

## Baseline Form
Intro (4-8) → Verse 1 (8-16) → Chorus (8) → Verse 2 (8-16) → Chorus (8) → Bridge (8) → Final Chorus + Tag.

## Best Practices
- Get to story premise immediately.
- Ensure each section changes knowledge or emotion.
- Reserve strongest line for final chorus/tag if possible.

## LLM Workflow
- Produce beat sheet and bar map together.
- Offer radio cut and writer-round acoustic cut.
- Add bridge alternatives (hopeful vs. devastating).

## Integration Notes
Cross-check section pacing with `../The Ultimate Country Songwriting Handbook – 2025 Edition.md`.

## Evaluation Rubric
- Narrative progression
- Structural efficiency
- Ending impact
