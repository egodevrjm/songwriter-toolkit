# Persona Prompting Guidelines for Songwriting LLMs

## Purpose
Define safe, effective persona prompting patterns for consistent creative outputs.

## Best Practices
- Specify persona role, era, influence set, and output objective.
- Keep persona instructions concise and behavior-focused.
- Separate persona constraints from formatting constraints.

## Recommended Prompt Skeleton
1. Persona identity and creative stance.
2. Task objective (lyrics, harmony, structure, production notes).
3. Style constraints (genre, tone, audience, explicitness).
4. Output schema (sections, bar counts, options).
5. Self-check rubric.

## Guardrails
- Avoid requests to imitate living artists verbatim.
- Ask for influence-level traits, not identity replication.
- Include safety and originality reminders.
