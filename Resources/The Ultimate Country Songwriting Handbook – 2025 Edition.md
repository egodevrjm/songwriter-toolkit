# The Ultimate Country Songwriting Handbook — 2025 Edition (Operational Checklist)

## Purpose
A fast, practical pre-demo checklist for writers and LLM pipelines.

## Non-Negotiables
- Title appears as a clear chorus anchor.
- Story premise is clear in Verse 1.
- Verse 2 adds new information.
- Bridge changes emotional perspective.
- Final chorus delivers strongest phrasing/tag.

## 2025 Radio/Streaming Checks
- Hook identity in first 10-15 seconds.
- No unnecessary intro drift.
- Runtime generally competitive with modern playlists.
- Chorus melody contour distinct from verse contour.
- One quotable line suitable for short-form clips.

## Revision Passes
1. **Truth pass:** remove any line you don’t believe.
2. **Clarity pass:** replace abstract nouns with images.
3. **Prosody pass:** align stressed syllables with downbeats.
4. **Trim pass:** cut weak words and filler syllables.
5. **Sing test:** perform front-to-back with minimal editing.
