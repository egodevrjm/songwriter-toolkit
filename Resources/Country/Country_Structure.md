# Country Structure Playbook

## Objective
Deliver narrative-driven songs with clear section purpose and strong chorus returns.

## Typical Form
- Intro: 4–8 bars
- Verse 1: 8–16 bars
- Chorus: 8 bars
- Verse 2: 8–16 bars
- Chorus: 8 bars
- Bridge: 8 bars
- Final chorus + tag: 8–16 bars

## Structural Principles
- Introduce story context immediately.
- Ensure each verse advances plot or emotional perspective.
- Use final tag to deliver memorable closing line.

## LLM Planning Guidance
Request:
- Beat sheet: what each section must reveal.
- Bar counts and rhyme scheme per section.
- Optional radio edit and acoustic session variants.
- Tag-line alternatives for final chorus.

## Quality Checklist
- Does Verse 2 add new meaning rather than repeat Verse 1?
- Is bridge a true turn, not filler?
- Is ending emotionally conclusive?
