# Hip-Hop Instrumentation Playbook

## Mission
Deliver hard-hitting, vocal-centric production with disciplined low-end management.

## Core Stack
- Kick/snare/hat groove engine.
- 808 or bass instrument with controlled sustain.
- Musical texture (sample/key/guitar/pad).
- FX and vocal textures for transitions.

## Best Practices
- Phase-check kick + bass.
- Keep center uncluttered for lead vocal.
- Humanize hats/perc velocity and timing.

## LLM Workflow
- Output drum pattern concepts by section.
- Provide frequency ownership map.
- Add mobile-speaker fallback notes.

## Evaluation Rubric
- Punch at low volume
- Sub consistency (30-80Hz)
- Vocal intelligibility in hook
