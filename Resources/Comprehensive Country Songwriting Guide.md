# Comprehensive Country Songwriting Guide (Integrated Edition)

## Purpose
Long-form strategy guide for writing country songs that are story-first, hook-forward, and LLM-ready.

## Country Fundamentals
- **Three chords and the truth:** emotional believability is the core metric.
- **Specificity creates universality:** concrete detail makes feelings relatable.
- **Melody + title hook:** chorus must be identifiable after one listen.

## Writing Workflow (Human + LLM)
1. Define protagonist, setting, and conflict in one paragraph.
2. Draft title/hook candidates (10-20).
3. Build chorus around emotional thesis.
4. Write Verse 1 with scene details.
5. Write Verse 2 with new stakes.
6. Use bridge for reversal, confession, or resolution.
7. Perform a conversational-language cleanup pass.

## Production & Arrangement Direction
- Traditional lane: acoustic, steel/fiddle, organic drum pocket.
- Modern lane: same storytelling, with pop/hip-hop-informed rhythmic polish.
- Keep vocal intelligibility as the top mix priority.

## How This Connects to `/Country`
Use this as a strategy layer and the `/Country` playbooks as execution templates for lyrics, harmony, structure, and instrumentation.
