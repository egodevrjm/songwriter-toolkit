# Pop-Country Songwriting Bible for LLMs (Production-Focused)

## Purpose
Define modern crossover constraints so generated songs can compete in streaming-era country-pop formats.

## Commercial Constraints (Practical Targets)
- Song length target: ~2:30 to ~3:10.
- Hook arrival target: ideally within first 40-50 seconds.
- Intro target: minimal, identity-forward.
- Tempo zone: mid-tempo friendly for playlist compatibility.

## Composition Blueprint
- Country storytelling in verses.
- Pop melodic economy in chorus.
- Optional hip-hop rhythmic influence in drums and vocal pocket.
- Bridge or breakdown should refresh energy quickly.

## LLM Prompt Template
"Write a modern pop-country song with:
- title-first chorus,
- verse scenes rooted in place,
- under-3:10 structure,
- one 8-15 second social-clip-ready hook phrase,
- instrumentation notes for acoustic+modern hybrid production."
