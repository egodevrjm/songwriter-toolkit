# Crafting Pop Hit Hooks and Words

## Purpose
Cross-genre hook engineering reference for Pop and Pop-country workflows.

## Hook Best Practices
- Keep primary hook phrase short (usually 3-8 words).
- Use high-contrast emotional language.
- Repeat with variation (melody, rhythm, harmony, or production).
- Favor open vowels on sustained/high notes.

## Word Choice Rules
- Prioritize concrete verbs and nouns.
- Avoid overly literary lines in chorus.
- Use one memorable conversational phrase listeners might quote.

## LLM Workflow
- Generate hook bank by emotion (euphoric, wistful, defiant, intimate).
- Score each hook by recall, singability, and uniqueness.
- Expand only top-scoring hooks into full chorus concepts.
